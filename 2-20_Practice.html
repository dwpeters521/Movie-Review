Plot

Software Engineer Peter Gibbons is at a crossroads in his life.
Should he continue working at a job he hates? Or should he try to get fired?
Take a hard look at the realities of 9-5 drudgery in this hilarious comedy by Mike Judge
And ask yourself, "Is today the worst day of your life?"
